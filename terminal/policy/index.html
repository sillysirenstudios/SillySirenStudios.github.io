Privacy Policy

Silly Siren Terminal  ·  Last updated: March 2026
Short version: Silly Siren Terminal collects no personal data. It has no analytics, no crash reporting, and no third-party SDKs. All network traffic goes directly from your device to servers you control.

Data Collection

Silly Siren Terminal does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account is required to use the app. Server credentials and SSH keys are stored locally on your device and never leave it except as part of direct SSH connections to servers you configure.

Network Connections

The app connects only to SSH servers that you explicitly add and connect to. All network traffic goes directly from your device to your server. No data is routed through Silly Siren Studios servers or any third-party infrastructure.

Analytics and Tracking

The app contains no analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind. We do not know how many people use the app, how often, or what they do with it.

In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. Silly Siren Studios does not receive or store your payment information. We have no visibility into who purchases Pro beyond what Apple provides in aggregate App Store reporting.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
